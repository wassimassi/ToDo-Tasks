Manage Your Tasks

Make your work easier with our Todo List App
How to Use
Add Task:

Type your task in the input field and click "Add" button or press Enter.

Edit Task:

Click anywhere on an uncompleted task row to edit. The row will highlight in yellow while editing.

Complete Task:

Click the green check button to mark a task as completed. Completed tasks cannot be edited.

Delete Task:

Click the red trash button to remove a task from the list.

Keyboard Shortcuts:

Press Enter to add/update task
Press Escape to cancel editing
